Welcome to Zephyr DMA Config Helper

Configuring DMA for STM32 peripherals in Zephyr is painful — hunting through reference manuals and CubeMX projects to find valid stream/channel/request-id combinations takes hours and is easy to get wrong.

This tool uses the STM32CubeMX database to automatically look up every valid DMA mapping for your peripheral and generate ready-to-use snippets:

  • A DTS overlay (dma.overlay) with the correct dmas and dma-names properties
  • A prj.conf snippet with the right Kconfig symbols for your DMA family (classic DMA or GPDMA)
  • A checks list of things to verify before you build

Supports all STM32 families · UART · SPI · MCU or board selection · share links
